About Me

Francis Rodson D. Clavel is a technical support professional living and working in Oakland, CA. Originally from Pittsburg, CA, he studied at the University of California, Berkeley earning his BA in Cognitive Science in 2015. He started working as part of the Application Support Engineering team at Adobe Advertising Cloud (fka Tubemogul) in 2015. There he serve as a remote member of the team, establishing a support system first in the company's New York office, then again at the company's London office before eventually returning to HQ in Emeryville, CA to become the team lead in 2018.

While at Adobe, he continues to work with product, dev and client services teams to troubleshoot, escalate and resolve customer issues. He has helped cross-functional teams drive testing efforts for new product features, resolve data discrepancies between Adobe and 3rd party partners, and create data reporting workflows for high profile clients.

As of September 2019, he is currently enrolled in the UC Berkeley Extension Full Stack Web Development Coding Bootcamp in Berkeley, CA.
